Aditya Prateek Singh[a] (born 19 November 1984), known professionally as Badshah (also stylised as badboyshah), is an Indian rapper, singer, songwriter, music producer and entrepreneur. Badshah began his career as an underground English rapper under the stage name "Cool Equal". Badshah has since made significant contributions to Bollywood soundtracks, with notable works in films such as Humpty Sharma Ki Dulhania, Veere Di Wedding, Namaste England among others.
